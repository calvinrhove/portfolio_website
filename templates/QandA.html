Q: Why did we found Twin Studios?

A: Twin Studios is an organization built to deploy artwork and tell stories that otherwise wouldn't be told due to profitability concerns

 

Q: Where did the name Twin Studios come from?

A: The name is a reference to the Twin Cities of Minneapolis and St. Paul, where both Reitsuka and Calvin grew up

 

Q: Who are the characters on the front page?

A: The left one is Lucy Schwartz, and the right is her alter ego Lucilia Shiro-Schwartz. Both are characters from the novel and game adaptation Lucilia: In a Black and White World

 

Q: Who is your favorite character?

Reitsuka: Remilila Lemoiux from A Human Reworked

Calvin: I should say Leo from Twin Souls, as he's obviously based on me, but my favorite is actually Kira from Life at 60 Frames per Second

 

Q: Which is your favorite work?

Reitsuka: A Human Reworked might be amateurish, but it's the best representation of the human spirit that I've ever made

Calvin: Life at 60 Frames Per Second without a doubt. Nothing comes close to how well all of the artistic elements align in this particular work
